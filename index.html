About Us

Peoria's Go-To Hauling & Moving Crew

Call N Haul Trailer Services LLC is a locally owned and operated business based in Peoria, AZ, proudly serving customers across nearly all of Arizona. We built this company on one simple idea: hauling and moving services should be easy to book, fairly priced, and actually show up when they say they will.

Whether you need junk cleared out, furniture moved across town, help loading a truck, or a same-day Facebook Marketplace pickup — we've got you covered. We're available 24/7 because we know life doesn't always run on a 9-to-5 schedule.
